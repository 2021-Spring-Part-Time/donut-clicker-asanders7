Donut Clicker

Click To Make Donuts!
×

Fred the Baker Baking Company is famous for producing a constant stream of delicious donuts to satisfy the public. Fred the Baker has decided to retire after an exhausting career in the food industry and wanted this game to be created so he can continue creating donuts. Donut Maker Clicker has now come from the drawing board to the browser. Help keep Fred's love of donuts alive and see how many donuts you can create!
